    About Us

    Down South CaJJun Eats was founded by twin brothers Jahmaal and Jermaine Dumes in the spring of 2017. While Studying at Southwestern University in Georgetown, TX we decided to take a leap of faith. Being from Beaumont, TX we felt that a huge part of our lives was missing in Austin. And that was Crawfish. Coming from a family of cooks, we’ve always dreamed of starting a food business. With the help and support of family and friends, we were able to make this dream come alive. We take pride in our authentic style of cooking and strive to give you the full Down South CaJJun experience with great food, great people, and a great time! Bringing the great taste of Southeast Texas/Louisiana to Austin!
